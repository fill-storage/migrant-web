# Accommodation

Finding a place to stay is one of the first steps when moving to a new country. Here are some tips and resources to help you find suitable accommodation:

## Types of Accommodation

- **Short-term rentals:** Hotels, hostels, serviced apartments, and Airbnb.
- **Long-term rentals:** Apartments, houses, shared accommodation.
- **Student housing:** Dormitories, university accommodation.

## Tips

- Research neighborhoods for safety and convenience.
- Check lease agreements carefully.
- Ask about utilities and internet.
- Consider proximity to work, school, and public transport.
